The Horse and His Boy by C.S. Lewis

Synopsis

The Horse and His Boy is the tale of a young boy named Shasta, a slave boy unfortunate circumstance, who goes on an adventure with a horse he meets named Bree. Shasta quickly learns that Bree is no ordinary horse, in fact, Bree is a talking horse from the magical land of Narnia! Shasta and Bree must work together to help one another escape their enslavement and reach the Narnia without being caught. Along the way they'll meet both friends and enemies, a wise magician, and a particular lion with a great purpose.
