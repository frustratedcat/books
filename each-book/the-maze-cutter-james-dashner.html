The Maze Cutter by James Dashner

Synopsis

The Maze Cutter is a book about forgettable, sociopathic characters who have no clue how to not act like complete idiots every second of every day. The story takes place decades after The Death Cure on the island where the Gladers escaped to. The morons hop on a ship called The Maze Cutter with a psycho lady and sail to California for stupid reasons that make no sense whatsoever. Things get a bit wild in Cali and random characters with no real purpose get added to the story. This book sucks, I will hate read James Dashner books forever.
