The Book of Dust Volume One: La Belle Sauvage by Philip Pullman

Synopsis

Volume One of The Book of Dust is named after a canoe but that never actually gets explained because Philip Pullman couldn't just take a paragraph to do that. The story is about a weird kid who sails about in his sketchy canoe in Britain. He has like three friends his own age and spends his time hanging out with nuns and a baby.
